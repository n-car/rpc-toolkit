# Code Of Conduct

RPC Toolkit projects should be professional, respectful, and focused on useful technical collaboration.

Participants are expected to:

- be respectful of differing experience levels and runtime backgrounds;
- discuss technical disagreements with concrete examples and evidence;
- avoid harassment, insults, personal attacks, and discriminatory language;
- keep issues and pull requests focused on the topic;
- avoid publishing private information or credentials.

Maintainers may moderate issues, pull requests, discussions, or other project spaces to keep collaboration productive and safe.
